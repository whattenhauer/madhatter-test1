January 31, 1995 - New moon

Sol Retreats:

Darkness prevails in its wake, Only will stars, that grasp break Thin silver sliver holds its station. Keeping faith; shouting out in quiet patience! New Beginnings!  New Creation! New Orb; silent cool to touch, Slips between warm sheets of bleeding sky. Crimson, cold, winter, wild, and clear; Unpolluted by the tears; Fear unabated. Wondering, forgiveness waiting, Will it come? New Moon! Rosh Chodesh! Your Choice!
